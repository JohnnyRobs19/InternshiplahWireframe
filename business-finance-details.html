Specializations:

Marketing:

Creative and analytical roles involving brand, ads, and social media.

  • Learn SEO, Google Analytics, Canva, and consumer behavior.
  • Take marketing strategy courses.
  • Apply on LinkedIn, through agency job boards, or directly on brand websites.
  • Prepare to discuss campaigns you've analyzed or improved. Know trends (e.g., TikTok strategy, consumer engagement).

Finance:

Involves analysis, investments, budgeting.

  • Learn Excel, financial modeling, and accounting basics. Take CFA Level 1 or Wall Street Prep if possible.
  • Use university portals, LinkedIn, and apply early (many roles recruit 6â€“12 months ahead).
  • Practice technical questions (e.g., DCF, balance sheet analysis). Use Vault/WSO guides.

Human Resources (HR):

Talent management, recruitment, training.

  • Study organizational behavior, labor law, and diversity topics. Build interpersonal and communication skills.
  • Apply via company HR portals, or attend HR-focused networking events.
  • Emphasize empathy, conflict resolution experience, and your understanding of company culture.

Consulting:

Problem-solving, client advising across industries.

  • Practice case studies, learn frameworks (e.g., SWOT, Porter's Five Forces), and take logic/reasoning tests.
  • Apply through consulting firm portals (McKinsey, BCG) and referral networks.
  • Master case interviews and behavioral questions; be structured and analytical in your answers.

Operations:

Logistics and sourcing.

  • Take operations management courses.
  • Apply via corporate career sites or manufacturing/logistics companies' hiring pages.
  • Be ready for scenario-based questions.
