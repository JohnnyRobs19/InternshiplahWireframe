Law Internships

🎯 Purpose

To assist Malaysian law students in navigating the internship landscape by providing firsthand experiences, preparation strategies, application guidance, and interview tips.

📖 Real Internship Stories

  • High Court Internships: Research skills, understanding court procedures, and drafting legal documents.
  • Law Firm Internships: Engaging in legal research, attending client meetings, and assisting in case preparations.
  • In-House Legal Departments: Exposure to compliance issues and contract reviews in corporate legal departments.

🛠️ How Students Prepare for Internships

  • Moot Court Competitions: Argumentation skills and legal research abilities.
  • Law Societies: Networking opportunities and insights into the legal profession.
  • Legal Aid Clinics: Practical experience in handling real cases and understanding client interactions.
  • Legal Writing: Contributing articles to legal blogs or journals enhances writing proficiency.
  • Networking: Attending legal seminars and workshops to connect with professionals.

🧠 Interview Tips: What to Expect

  • "Why did you choose to study law?"
  • "Which area of law interests you the most and why?"
  • "Can you discuss a recent legal issue that caught your attention?"

Preparation Strategies: Stay informed, do mock interviews, and understand the firm's background.

📝 How to Apply for Internships

  • Resume and Cover Letter: Customize applications to highlight relevant experiences.
  • LinkedIn Profile: Optimize with academic achievements, internships, and skills.
  • Platforms: JobStreet, Hiredly, and LegalJobsMalaysia are great platforms for finding internships.

📊 Relevant Statistics

  • Over 21,570 practicing lawyers in Malaysia (as of February 2023).
  • 37 large law firms with more than 30 lawyers offering internship opportunities.

📌 Recommendations for the Website

  • Dedicated Law Section with stories and resources.
  • Sample Documents: Resumes, cover letters, and LinkedIn profiles.
  • Interactive Q&A with former interns and legal professionals.
